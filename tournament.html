Falcon Free Fire Achievements

The term "Falcon Free Fire achievements" refers to two things: the in-game achievement system in Garena Free Fire, and the competitive achievements of Falcon FF in tournaments.

In-Game Achievements in Free Fire

  • Dominator: Achieve high rank with a certain number of kills.
  • Best Pal: Revive teammates a specified number of times.
  • Sharpshooter: Achieve kills from long distance (over 30m).
  • Wrestler: Secure close-combat kills (within 30m in BR or 10m in CS).
  • Peacemaker: Finish Top 2 in Squads or Top 3 in Duos with minimal kills.
  • Ninja: High finish with low movement while securing kills.
  • Guerrilla: High finish with high movement and multiple kills.
  • Chainkiller: In Clash Squad, multiple Triple/Quad Kills in one match.
  • Early Bird: In Clash Squad, get the first kill in multiple rounds.
